Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

1. Overview

The BS Machine (bsmachine.dev) is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information. We collect only what is necessary to operate the site and the tipping competition.

2. What We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your:

  • Email address — used to create and manage your account, and to contact you if you win a prize.
  • Display name / username — shown on the leaderboard and tipping pages.
  • Password — stored securely and hashed by our authentication provider (Supabase). We never see or store your plain-text password.

Tipping Data

Your match tips, submission timestamps, and scores are stored to run the tipping competition and display leaderboard rankings.

Usage Data (Analytics)

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site. This collects anonymised data such as pages visited, time on site, and general geographic region. This data cannot identify you personally. You can opt out via your browser's privacy settings or a browser extension such as the Google Analytics Opt-out Add-on.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information solely to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Run the tipping competition and display leaderboard standings
  • Contact you if you win a prize and to deliver that prize
  • Send transactional emails (e.g. email verification, password reset)

We do not send marketing or promotional emails. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the site:

  • Supabase — our database and authentication provider. Your email address and account data are stored on Supabase's infrastructure. Supabase is SOC 2 Type II certified. See their privacy policy at supabase.com/privacy.
  • Google Analytics — anonymised site usage analytics. See Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Render — our backend hosting provider. See their privacy policy at render.com/privacy.

5. Data Retention

Your account data and tipping history are retained for as long as your account remains active. If you request account deletion, your personal data (email address, display name, and tipping history) will be removed within 30 days.

6. Your Rights

Under Australian privacy law (the Privacy Act 1988), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated personal data

To exercise any of these rights, please email us at broncossabres@gmail.com.

7. Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. Communication between your browser and our services is encrypted via HTTPS. However, no system is completely secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the site constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

Questions or concerns about this privacy policy can be directed to broncossabres@gmail.com.

About the BS Machine

What is this site?

The BS Machine is a data-driven platform for analysing NRL team performance, projecting outcomes, and visualising each team's season trajectory.

How does it work?

The model uses a Bayesian approach to predict the expected score for each match based on the offensive and defensive ratings of the teams involved. After each round, the predicted result is compared to the actual outcome, and the ratings are updated accordingly. This update reflects both offensive and defensive performance, allowing the model to understand not just how strong a team is overall, but whether that strength comes from attack, defence, or both. The result is a dynamic rating system that captures team performance above or below average, split into offensive, defensive, and total rating components.

What data do you use?

Limitations

The model doesn't currently explicitly account for injuries, team announcements, or mid-season coaching changes. While accurate in trends, it's not meant to replace expert judgment.
